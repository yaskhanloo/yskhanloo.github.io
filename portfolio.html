Keyword Extraction Tool – Built a modular annotation tool to label key medical concepts and train weakly-supervised clinical models.

Imaging AI & Analysis

Brain Tissue Mapping at 7T – Co-developed a novel phase-cycled BSSFP technique for joint susceptibility, T1, and T2 quantification, now published and presented internationally.

Healthcare Data Infrastructure

Quality Monitoring Dashboard – Built a dashboard system to track data consistency, missing values, and variable formats across large-scale clinical datasets.
