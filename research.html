Research & Publications

Bridging Physics, Data Science, and Healthcare Innovation

Research Interests

My research focuses on the intersection of data science, physics, and biomedical applications. I am particularly interested in developing computational methods for medical imaging analysis, applying machine learning techniques to healthcare data, and leveraging statistical modeling for clinical decision support.

Current Research Areas

Medical Imaging & AI: Developing automated analysis pipelines for cardiac MRI data, focusing on improving diagnostic accuracy and workflow efficiency in clinical settings.

Healthcare Analytics: Applying advanced statistical methods and machine learning algorithms to large-scale healthcare datasets to identify patterns and improve patient outcomes.

Physics-Based Modeling: Utilizing physics principles and mathematical modeling to understand complex biological systems and improve medical imaging techniques.
